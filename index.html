Let's Learn More About Sushi 🍣

Sushi Types

NIGIRI
Raw fish, egg, veggies, or shrimp placed over sushi rice

MAKI SUSHI
Raw or cooked fish, veggies, and sushi rice wrapped in seaweed

URAMAKI
Raw or cooked fish, veggies, and seaweed wrapped in sushi rice

CRIPSY RICE SUSHI
Deep fried sushi rice topped with various sushi filling

HAND ROLLS
Raw or cooked fish, veggies, and sushi rice wrapped in seaweed in a cone shape

OSHIZUSHI
Created by taking a maki or uramaki roll and pressing it into a square shaped mold
